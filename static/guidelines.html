General

In return for being part of the clan and receiving clan perks the following guidelines are in place:

  • Clan wars in/out to be set on your profile at all times
  • If you indicate you are 'in' then you must use all available attacks
  • Donate to members clan castles lower down on war map
  • Attack with a full army including clan castle troops and siege machines
  • Your first attack should be your mirror; if your mirror has already been defeated then you can choose your target
  • Elder awarded/revoked for participation in war
  • You may invite your alt to the clan if it is a non-rushed TH13 or better; TH13 accounts will be used to boost war numbers and are not considered full member accounts. If the clan is full some may be requested to leave temporarily.
  • The clan manager records your donations even if you leave and return. Donations for the current month and the previous month are listed beside your promotion level and availability status. The first number is the current donations and the number in brackets are donations given prior to leaving the clan and coming back for the respective month.
In order to maintain a healthy clan, warnings will be given for the following
  • Failure to attack in war
  • Poor attacking performance
Warnings stay in effect for a week; three strikes and you will be removed from the clan

Clan War Leagues

  • Minimum requirement Elder and TH13
  • Fighting spots given based on clan rank
  • Heroes to be up during league
  • Clan castles to be filled with Max level troops (Ice Golem/Headhunters/Super Dragon/Super Minons)
  • Players will be replaced if they don't attack or attack without heros

Ranking Calculation

  • Clan members sorted by rank updated automatically via API
  • Maintains history for last 8 wars - excluding league wars
  • Rank = 100 per war attack with modifiers
    • +1 per 100 donated - will roll over into the beginning of the next season
    • +10 per star
    • 0-25 for destruction percentage
    • 20% more star & destruction bonus if defending TH is a higher level
    • 75% of star & destruction bonus if attacking 3 or more lower on the map
If you fail to attack in CWL or attack without heroes then -500 rank is applied until the end of the next CWL
A green arrow indicates your rank is improving based on the last three wars

CWL Rank

CWL bonuses are awarded based on the 'mod' column of the player table, which can be found here. The following CWL ranking calculation is used
  • + 30
  • + (3 * Attack Stars)
  • - Defense Stars
  • + The number of rounds you are fighting
  • + Donation modifier based on the previous months donations up to +6

Ore Farming

There is a secondary feeder clan available, which is used for farming ore during CWL. You can join the clan by clicking here. Once the CWL sign up period has ended notifications will be given in game so you can move over and sign up for a normal war thereby doing both CWL and normal war in parallel.

Choosing Targets

First attack should always be your mirror, if your mirror has been defeated you are free to choose your target using the following guidelines in order of importance
  1. Obtaining stars; if you can reasonably be expected to get stars then you should
  2. Attack as high up the map as you can; You certainly shouldn't be attacking more than three castles below unless there is not a reasonable chance of obtaining a star higher up the map
  3. Any castle if there is less than four hours remaining in the war and you can obtain multiple stars
